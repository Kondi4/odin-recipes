Greek-Style Potatoes

Description

These Greek potatoes are simple to make and go very well with chicken.

Ingredients

  • 6 potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 and half of a cup - water
  • Third of a cup - olive oil
  • Quarter of a cup - fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves - garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 cubes - chicken bouillon
  • 1 teaspoon - dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon - dried rosemary
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Arrange potatoes evenly in the bottom of a medium baking dish.
  3. Mix together water, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, bouillon cubes, thyme, rosemary, and pepper in a small bowl. Pour mixture over potatoes.
  4. Cover and bake in the preheated oven, turning occasionally, until potatoes are tender yet firm, 1.5 to 2 hours.
